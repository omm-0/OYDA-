About OYDA

Technology Partners, Not Just Vendors

We exist to prove that premium digital solutions aren't reserved for Fortune 500 companies. Every ambitious business deserves world-class technology.

The Beginning

OYDA was born from frustration. Our founder, Omar Al-Farsi, spent 12 years working inside Fortune 500 companies watching them invest millions in cutting-edge digital infrastructure — while the small businesses he admired most were stuck with templates, freelancers who disappeared mid-project, and agencies that treated them as afterthoughts.

The gap was clear: small and medium businesses generate 90% of the world's employment but receive less than 10% of its best technology talent. OYDA was founded to close that gap.

The Mission

We believe every business — regardless of size — deserves digital solutions engineered to the same standards that power Google, Stripe, and Airbnb. Not template websites. Not copy-paste chatbots. Not generic marketing campaigns. Real, custom-engineered technology built by senior professionals who care about outcomes, not just deliverables.

Our name says it all: Own Your Digital Application. We don't build things you rent from us — we build things you own completely. Every line of code, every design file, every strategic document becomes your intellectual property.

The Approach

We operate differently by design. We deliberately limit our active projects to ensure every client receives our full attention. We staff every project with senior engineers — never juniors learning on your dime. We put delivery guarantees in writing. And we integrate AI throughout our workflow — not to replace human creativity, but to amplify it, delivering 3x faster without compromising quality.

The result? A 98% client retention rate, zero missed deadlines across 50+ projects, and partnerships that last years, not months.

The Future

We're just getting started. Our vision is to become the most trusted digital transformation partner for ambitious SMBs worldwide. Currently serving clients across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, India, and the UK — we're expanding our reach while maintaining the boutique attention that defines the OYDA experience.

Every empire starts with a foundation. We're here to build yours.

What We Stand For

Principles that guide every decision

01

Quality Over Quantity

We deliberately limit active projects. Every client gets our full, undivided attention. We'd rather do 10 exceptional projects than 100 mediocre ones.

02

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees, no jargon, no surprises. We share our process, our progress, and our challenges openly. You always know exactly where things stand.

03

Ownership Mentality

We don't treat your project like just another ticket. We treat it like our own product — obsessing over details, optimizing performance, and caring about outcomes.

04

Continuous Learning

Technology evolves daily. So do we. Our team dedicates 20% of their time to learning new technologies, experimenting with emerging tools, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

05

Results Over Resumes

We don't hire based on pedigree — we hire based on craft. Our team is judged by the quality of their work and the impact they create, not the logos on their LinkedIn.

06

Long-Term Partnerships

We're not interested in one-and-done projects. We build relationships that span years. 80% of our revenue comes from repeat clients and referrals.

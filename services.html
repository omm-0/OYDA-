Service Questions

Everything you need to know

Which service is right for my business? +
It depends on your goals. If you need online presence, start with Web Development. If your audience is mobile-first, go with Mobile Apps. If you want to automate operations, AI is the answer. Not sure? Book a free discovery call and we'll recommend the perfect starting point based on your business model, budget, and timeline.
Can I combine multiple services? +
Absolutely — and most clients do. For example, a common combination is Web Development + UI/UX Design + SEO. Or Mobile App + AI Chatbot + Cloud Infrastructure. We offer bundled pricing for multi-service projects that typically saves 15-20% compared to separate engagements.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance? +
Yes. Every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support. After that, we offer monthly retainer packages starting at $500/month covering bug fixes, updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and feature additions. About 80% of our clients opt for ongoing support.
What if I need changes after the project is complete? +
Changes within the agreed scope during development are unlimited — we iterate until you're satisfied. Post-launch changes are covered during the 30-day support period. Beyond that, changes are handled through our retainer packages or quoted as separate mini-projects.
Do you work with startups or only established businesses? +
We work with both. About 40% of our clients are startups building their first digital product, and 60% are established businesses scaling their digital presence. We adjust our approach based on your stage — lean MVPs for startups, robust enterprise solutions for established companies.
How do you handle intellectual property? +
Simple: you own everything. Every line of code, every design file, every document we create becomes your property upon final payment. We provide complete source code, design files, documentation, and deployment credentials. No vendor lock-in, ever.

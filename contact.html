Before You Reach Out

Do I need a detailed brief before contacting you? +
Not at all. Some of our best projects started as 'I have an idea but don't know where to start.' That's literally what our discovery phase is for. Come as you are.
Is the initial consultation free? +
Yes, always. The first discovery call (30-45 minutes) is completely free with no obligations. We'll give you genuine strategic advice regardless of whether you hire us.
Do you sign NDAs before the first call? +
If you'd like, absolutely. Just mention it in your message and we'll have a mutual NDA signed before the call. Your ideas are safe with us.
What should I prepare for the discovery call? +
Come with your business goals, target audience description, any competitor examples you admire, and a rough budget range. If you have existing brand guidelines or technical documentation, share those too. But honestly, just showing up with your vision is enough.
Can I get a ballpark quote before the call? +
We can provide rough ranges based on project type: websites ($200K-1000K), mobile apps ($8K-80K), AI solutions ($6K-50K), marketing ($2K-10K/month). Exact pricing requires a proper scope discussion.
What's your minimum project budget? +
Our minimum engagement is $200. This typically covers focused projects like a marketing website, brand identity package, or a simple chatbot integration.

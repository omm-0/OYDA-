Our Hiring Process

Transparent, respectful, and fast

Step 1

Application Review

2-3 days

Submit your resume and portfolio. We review every application personally — no AI screening.

Step 2

Culture Chat

30 min

A casual conversation about your background, interests, and what you're looking for. No trick questions.

Step 3

Technical Assessment

2-3 hours

A practical take-home challenge relevant to the role. We evaluate problem-solving approach, not just output.

Step 4

Final Interview & Offer

45 min + 1-2 days

Meet the team lead. Discuss the challenge. If it's a mutual fit, you'll have an offer within 48 hours.
